## 15 – Annexes

### How to Read This Chapter
Annexes provide optional tools for implementation. They are supporting instruments, not required workflows, and their use is governed by the core framework.

### 15.1 Purpose
Provide legal, operational, governance, and methodological instruments.

The purpose statement frames why annexes exist; the structure below clarifies what each annex contains.


### 15.2 Structure
Each annex includes:
- title,
- purpose,
- scope,
- instructions,
- compatibility,
- licensing.

With structure defined, the following list enumerates the annexes included in VILF 1.0.
