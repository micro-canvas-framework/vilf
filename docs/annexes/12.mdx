# Annex 12 — Hub Capability Development Playbook

## 12.1 Purpose
This annex offers a structured capability development playbook for Hubs (Tier-1).  
It guides systematic maturity progression across labs and supports national or institutional scaling.

## 12.2 Capability Domains (Aligned with IMM)

1. **Strategy & Alignment**  
2. **Governance & Risk**  
3. **Execution & Experimentation**  
4. **Documentation & Evidence**  
5. **Learning & Collaboration**

## 12.3 Standard Capability Pathway

### Stage 1 — Initial
- Training on IMM-P® basics  
- Fundamentals of documentation  
- Introduction to IGF  

### Stage 2 — Emerging
- Improved experimentation capability  
- Early MEL literacy  
- Structured research practices  

### Stage 3 — Developing
- Competent prototyping  
- Portfolio-level synthesis  
- Effective normalization  

### Stage 4 — Established
- Advanced maturity assessments  
- Governance advisory  
- Cross-lab support programs  

### Stage 5 — Strategic
- Design of system-level capabilities  
- Innovation policy influence  
- Leading multi-sector collaborations  

## 12.4 Hub-Led Training Modules

### Module 1 — IMM-P® Execution  
Focus: Stage clarity, evidence artifacts, research discipline.

### Module 2 — Governance & IGF  
Focus: Pathways, escalation, documentation rules.

### Module 3 — MEL & Scorecard  
Focus: KPIs, weighting, evidence linkages.

### Module 4 — Prototyping & Experimentation  
Focus: Rapid iteration, hypothesis testing.

### Module 5 — Strategic Alignment  
Focus: MCF integration, institutional anchoring.

## 12.5 Output Templates
- Capability development plans  
- Maturity progression maps  
- Training attendance logs  
- Module evaluation forms
