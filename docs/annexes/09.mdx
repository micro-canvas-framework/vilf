## 09.1 Purpose
This annex provides a standardized intake and prioritization format for institutions participating in VILF.  
It ensures that innovation challenges enter the system in a structured, evidence-oriented manner.

## 09.2 Intake Form Template

### Section A — Challenge Overview
- Title  
- Institution  
- Department / Unit  
- Contact person  
- Summary of challenge  

### Section B — Problem Definition (MCF-Aligned)
- Strategic context  
- Problem dynamics  
- Evidence of relevance  
- Existing alternatives  
- Impact scope  

### Section C — Expected Outcomes
- Desired change  
- Target users  
- Expected benefits  
- Strategic alignment score (1–5)  

### Section D — Constraints
- Time constraints  
- Regulatory constraints  
- Resource constraints  
- Data constraints  

### Section E — Readiness (IMM-Aligned)
- Institutional readiness level  
- Stakeholder commitment  
- Availability of data  
- Governance stability  

### Section F — Prioritization Criteria
Scores (1–5):
- Impact potential  
- Feasibility  
- Alignment with strategy  
- Innovation opportunity  
- Evidence availability  

### Section G — Recommendation
- Accept into IMM-P®? (Yes/No)  
- Recommended Lab (Tier-0)  
- Recommended Hub (Tier-1)  
- Notes and next steps
